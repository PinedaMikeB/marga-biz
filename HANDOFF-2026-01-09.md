## PROJECT OVERVIEW

### What is this project?
Migration of marga.biz from WordPress (Hostinger) to a static HTML/JS site hosted on Netlify. The goal was to preserve SEO rankings (#2 for "printer rental" in Philippines) while gaining faster load times and lower hosting costs.

### Business Context
Marga Enterprises is a printer/copier rental business serving Metro Manila and surrounding provinces. The website is a key lead generation tool.

## SESSION NOTES

### SSL Issue Resolved
The SSL certificate wouldn't provision because of an AAAA (IPv6) record pointing to old Hostinger server. Netlify doesn't support IPv6. Deleting the AAAA record fixed the issue.

### Two Folders Clarification
- `marga-biz/` = The actual website (deployed to Netlify)
- `Marga-website/` = Migration toolkit (browser-based tools, not deployed)
